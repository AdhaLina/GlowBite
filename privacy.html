Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026
Short version: GlowBite collects only what it needs to work — your name, email and photo from Google if you sign in. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or track you across other websites. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.
Section 1

Who we are

GlowBite is a skin nutrition app that helps you discover foods for glowing skin. It is operated by the GlowBite team (adharalina@gmail.com).

For the purposes of UK and EU data protection law (UK GDPR / EU GDPR), GlowBite is the data controller — meaning we decide how and why your personal data is used.

Section 2

What data we collect

We only collect data that is necessary for the app to function. Here is exactly what we collect and why:

Data Source Why we collect it
Full name Google account To personalise your greeting in the app
Email address Google account To identify your account and for account recovery
Profile photo Google account To display your avatar in the app
Saved foods Your actions in the app To remember your favourite glow foods
Skin journal entries Your actions in the app To track your skin journey over time
Reminders Your actions in the app To send you glow check-in notifications
Glow activity Your actions in the app To calculate your weekly progress chart

If you choose to continue without signing in, we collect no personal data at all. Your saved foods and journal are stored only on your device and never sent to our servers.

Section 3

What we do NOT collect

  • Your Google password — we never see it. Google handles authentication entirely.
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts, camera or microphone
  • Any health or medical information beyond what you choose to write in your own skin journal
  • Browsing history or data from other websites or apps
  • Payment card details — these are handled entirely by Stripe and never touch our servers
Section 4

Legal basis for processing (UK & EU GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

Processing activity Legal basis
Google sign-in and account creation Contract — necessary to provide the service you signed up for
Saving your foods, journal and progress Contract — core functionality of the app
Sending reminders Consent — you explicitly set these up yourself
Premium subscription billing Contract — necessary to process your payment
Section 5

How we store your data

Your account data is stored securely on Supabase — a cloud database provider that is SOC 2 certified. All data is encrypted at rest and all connections use HTTPS encryption in transit.

Data stored on your device (saved foods, journal, activity) uses your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you are signed in, in which case it is synced to your secure Supabase account.

We do not store your data on any server we personally own or operate. Supabase's infrastructure is hosted on AWS in the region you were closest to when you signed up.

Section 7

How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as you have an active GlowBite account. If you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted from our database within 30 days.

Data stored in your browser's localStorage is kept on your device until you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.

Section 8

Your rights (UK & EU GDPR)

Under UK and EU data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete all your data at any time ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to stop using your data while keeping it stored
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — you can object to how we use your data
  • Right to withdraw consent — where we rely on consent (e.g. reminders), you can withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at adharalina@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the UK at ico.org.uk, or your local data protection authority in the EU.

Section 9

Cookies and local storage

GlowBite does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

We use localStorage — a browser feature that stores data on your device — to save your glow foods, journal entries, reminders and settings. This data stays on your device and is not used for tracking or advertising.

When you sign in with Google, Supabase sets a session token in localStorage to keep you signed in. This is a functional necessity, not a tracking cookie.

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how many people visit GlowBite. It is privacy-first and does not use any cookies, does not fingerprint you, and does not collect any personal or identifiable data — it only counts anonymous page views.

Section 10

Children's privacy

GlowBite is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with their data, please contact us at adharalina@gmail.com and we will delete it immediately.

Users aged 13–17 should have a parent or guardian review this privacy policy before using GlowBite.

Section 11

Health disclaimer

GlowBite provides general wellness and nutrition information for educational purposes only. Nothing in the app constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it is not a substitute for consultation with a qualified doctor or dermatologist.

Always seek professional medical advice before making significant dietary changes, using food-based skincare if you have a skin condition or allergies, or if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or managing a medical condition. You use GlowBite's suggestions at your own discretion and risk.

Continuing to use GlowBite after a policy update means you accept the updated policy.
